2nd Level:
Air + Earth = Sand (or Dust)
Air + Fire = Radiation (no, not radio-activity)
Air + Water = Mist
Earth + Fire = Glass
Earth + Water = Mud
Fire + Water = Steam

3th Level:
Air + 2x Earth = Stone
Fire + 2x Earth = Metal
Water + 2x Earth = Wood

Earth + 2x Air = Sound
Fire + 2x Air = Plasma
Water + 2x Air = Lightning (or Storm)

Air + 2x Water = Ice
Earth + 2x Water = Life
Fire + 2x Water = Poison

Air + 2x Fire = Death
Earth + 2x Fire = Lava
Water + 2x Fire = Pressure
